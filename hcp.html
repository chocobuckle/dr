About DuoResp Spiromax®

  • DuoResp Spiromax contains budesonide and formoterol*
  • Dry powder inhaler
  • Two strengths:
  • Two strengths therapeutically equivalent to Symbicort (budesonide/formoterol) Turbohaler®1
  • Similar safety profile to Symbicort Turbohaler1
  • Licensed for asthma and COPD2
  • Maintenance and Reliever Therapy (MART) licence in asthma2**

If you would like to know more then please request a representative visit.
